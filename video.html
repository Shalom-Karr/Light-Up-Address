About this demo

What you're seeing.

This is an AddressIt LED illuminated house number sign by Illumicor, captured at dusk on a real Cleveland Heights home. The sign turns on automatically as ambient light drops — no switch, no timer, no app.

AddressIt LED makes two series of light-up address signs: the MDR backlit signs where the number glows from behind a black or white backplate, and the IBL 3D dimensional numbers that stand out off the wall with optional color-temperature illumination.

Every sign includes a built-in dusk-to-dawn sensor, mounting hardware, and a 3-year warranty. Weatherproof and tested for Northeast Ohio winters.
