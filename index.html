Questions

Good questions, real answers.

What's the difference between MDR and IBL? +

MDR signs are lit backplates — the number (and optional street name) is illuminated from behind on a black or white backplate. White or red illumination, Futura or Iowan Old font. IBL signs are 3D physical numbers in black, silver, or white — sold as single digits (IBL), numbers on a line (IBLL), or numbers on a plate (IBLP), with optional color-temperature illumination.

Solar or hardwired? +

Two power options: a plug-in adaptor (works with any standard outlet) or hardwired into existing exterior lighting. Built-in dusk-to-dawn sensor either way.

Will it work in Cleveland winters? +

Yes — weatherproof and tested for Northeast Ohio winters.

How bright is it? Will it bother neighbors? +

Bright enough to read from across the street, not bright enough to act as a porch light.

When and how do I pay? +

No payment is collected on this site. After you submit, Illumicor calls you within one business day to confirm and take payment by phone. The charge appears on your statement as ILLUMICOR. A minimal foreign transaction fee may apply depending on your bank.

Warranty? +

3 years on every sign. Anything breaks, Illumicor replaces it.

Can I change my order after submitting? +

These are custom-built signs. Once production begins, the order can't be changed or cancelled — so double-check your number, font, and color before you submit.
