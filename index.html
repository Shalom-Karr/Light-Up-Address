Questions

Good questions, real answers.

How does it stay charged? +

An integrated solar panel charges the internal battery during the day. A full charge runs the sign through 3 cloudy days. There's no plug, wiring, or replaceable battery.

Will it work in winter? +

Yes. Rated to -20°F. We've tested it through Minnesota winters. Snow on the panel reduces charge time, but as long as it gets some sun every few days it'll keep running.

How bright is it? Will it bother neighbors? +

Bright enough to read clearly from 50 feet — not bright enough to act as a porch light. Soft warm-white LEDs, designed to be visible without being a nuisance.
